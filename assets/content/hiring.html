Job title: CVAD IT Student Position (Lvl-2)

Position type: On Campus

CVAD is hiring Information Technology Student Employees (LVL-2). These positions are part of our service desk, providing support to our instructors and students.

This position will be included as a member of our Innovation Technology Group and will have the opportunity to contribute to the development of new solutions and applications.

The ideal candidate will have some familiarity, and/or a willingness to continue to learn, in the following areas:

  • Windows, MacOS, and Linux Operating systems.
  • Networking principles.
  • Information Security practices.
  • Git workflow.
  • Proficient in coding with: JavaScript, Python, PHP among others.
  • Open-Source software solutions.

Experience with the Linux is a plus.

Experience with Python is a plus.

Experience with the Raspberry Pi is a plus.

All CVAD IT student employees will have Service Desk responsibilities: working directly with end-users, providing support in instructional spaces, documenting solutions, responding to tickets, service monitoring, and deploying and testing new equipment and services.

This position will be responsible for performing tasks in the CVAD Building: the CVAD Service Desk, CVAD Student Computer Lab, and the CVAD Digital Fabrication Lab. Assisting in other areas will be required when needed.

Applicants should possess strong communication skills (verbal and written) and work well with others as they will be required to assist faculty and students.
