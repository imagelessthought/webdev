Webrinting Using PaperCut

There are printers available to employees, students, and guests in the CVAD Art Building that do not require anything to be installed on your computer or device as long as you are connected to the internet. You simply login to the PaperCut Webportal with your EUID and password to submit your print job.

Here is information on connecting to the Campus Wi-Fi Network.

File Format Restrictions

There are file restrictions for webprint. The file to be printed must be of one of the following file formats:

  • Microsoft Excel documents: xlam, xls, xlsb, xlsm, xlsx, xltm, xltx.
  • Microsoft PowerPoint documents: pot, potm, potx, ppam, pps, ppsm, ppsx, ppt, pptm, pptx.
  • Microsoft Word documents: doc, docm, docx, dot, dotm, dotx.
  • Basic text files: rtf, txt.
  • PDF documents: pdf.
  • Image files: bmp, dib, gif, jfif, jif, jpe, jpeg, jpg, png, tif, tiff.

Employees

CVAD employees may submit web print jobs to the Ricoh Copier\Printer and the HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M751DN printer located in ART 235. Art 235 requires swipe card access, if your ID card does not open this space, please contact our office. The Ricoh Copier\Printer located in the Dean's Office is also available but should only be used by those not working in the this area if the options in ART 235 are not available for some reason.

  • When a print job is submitted to one of the Ricoh Printer-Copiers, the job will be queued, and you will need to login to the copier once you arrive to release the print job. This is done to reduce the number of print jobs that are not picked up and to provide protection for confidential, private, and sensitive materials.
  • The HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M751DN does not use a print\release option, print jobs will automatically begin printing once submitted.

    Do not print confidential, private, or sensitive materials on this printer as it is not actively monitored
  • When printing remotely, be sure to use unique filenames for easy identification (for example: use your last name-document name, like ''williams-syllabus'').

Submitting a Job for Web Printing

  1. Login to the PaperCut Webportal with your EUID and password.
  2. Click on Web Print in the left-hand menu.
  3. Click Submit a Job.
  4. Enter ''cvad'' into the search box and click the green arrow to find the CVAD printer you wish to submit your print to.
  5. Then click Print Options and Account Selection. The location column will identify where the printer is located in the building.
  6. Specify the number of copies desired, then click Upload Documents.
  7. Either drag and drop files into the upload box or click Upload from computer to select the files for printing. Make note of the file type restrictions. If the document is not one of the listed types, convert the document to a PDF prior to printing. NOTE: Contact our service desk if you need assistance with file conversion.
  8. Once the desired files are selected, click Upload & Complete. Your print job has now been sent to the print queue.
  9. See Releasing Print Jobs from the Ricoh MP4055 below.

Releasing Print Jobs from the Ricoh MP4055

  1. At the printer, tap the PaperCut MF icon to access the Print Release screen.
  2. Press the ID number button. If you can’t remember your eight-digit Employee ID Number, you can choose Username and password to use your EUID and UNT password.
  3. Tap ?123 to switch to numeric entry and enter your eight-digit Employee ID Number. Then tap Log in.
  4. The next screen will show how many jobs you have in the queue. To release all of your jobs, tap Print all. Otherwise, tap Print release to see a list of your jobs in the queue. NOTE: You will only see your jobs when you view the queue, you will not see any other users’ jobs, nor will any other users see your jobs.
  5. If you chose to view your queue, select the desired jobs, and tap Print.
  6. Be sure to click Log out and return to the Home screen when finished.
