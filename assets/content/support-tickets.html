Getting Help: Submitting Requests and Reporting Issues

To report an incident or request support, please submit a ticket using the link below.

Submit a Ticket ⊳ ServiceNow

Issues that hinder an instructor's ability to teach effectively are given the highest priority at the College of Visual Arts and Design. If you are experiencing an issue in one of our instructional spaces during a scheduled class period, please use the link to submit a ticket and a technician will be dispatched to your location as quickly as possible to resolve the issue so that the impact on instruction is minimized.

The campus uses ServiceNow for our ticketing and call tracking. You will be asked to login before submitting a ticket.

If you have a question, you may email our service desk : but if you need to report an incident or make a request we will need you to submit a ticket.

End User Support

If you would like to receive immediate support during normal business hours, please visit our Online ServiceDesk and one of our technicians will begin a remote support session with you. If the issue is less time sensitive, you may also submit a ticket.
