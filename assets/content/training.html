There are several online training options available to UNT students, faculty, and staff. Below is information on each along with a brief description of what is available. The options below are all provided free of charge to you as part of a system or campus agreement. We will regularly add training resources that may be relevant to our students, faculty, and staff. Unless otherwise noted, all resources below are maintained by UNT.

General Training Resources Available

UNT Bridge

UNT Bridge is the primary online training option for university employees. The content is primarily focused on business processes and compliance training. This is where the Annual Cybersecurity Awareness Training is hosted. Some other examples: UNT Ethics and Standards of Conduct, ePRO Coordinator Training, Anti-Bias & Cultural Awareness Training, and the UNT Policies & Title IX: Preventing Discrimination, Harassment, Sexual Misconduct & Retaliation Training.

Linked-in-Learning

The College of Visual Arts and Design was responsible for bringing Linked-In-Learning to the UNT Campus. Here you will find a variety of content, but the bulk of their training modules are software-related. Instructors can assign playlists to their students and track their performance and completion status. Supervisors can do the same for their staff. If you have any trouble setting up and sharing a playlist, or anything else, please let us know.
