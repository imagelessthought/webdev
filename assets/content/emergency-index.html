Emergency Procedures Information

In the event of an emergency situation (medical, fire or police) the appropriate action is to immediately call 911.

  • For NON-EMERGENCIES, you may contact the UNT Police Department on their 24-Hour Non-Emergency Line: (940) 565-3000.
  • To report a facilities-related issue, you may contact UNT Work Control: (940) 565-2700.
