Exhibitions and Events: Slideshows for Displays

College of Visual Arts and Design

If you have an upcoming exhibition or event that requires an image slideshow, we provide a service that may meet your needs: we can create and host your slideshow on our webserver so that it is accessible using any web browser. All of the elements of the slideshow are customizable. You can choose the slide display durations and other design elements. Once we have worked with you to create your slide show we will send you a custom URL that you can use on any digital display simply by opening a web browser to that page and putting the browser in full screen mode. We can provide digital display and projectors for your exhibition or event: you can reserve the equipment you will need through our Equipment Checkout System.

This service does not currently support video in the slideshows but that is a feature planned for the Fall 2023.

Example Slideshows

The slideshows can be customized to meet your needs. Below are some examples slideshows. The captions used in the examples are the defaults but you can choose the font, font-color, and position of the caption.

When using the links below, you will need to switch your browser to full screen mode to get the full effect and see how they will appear for your exhibition and event.

1:Digital Sign: This autoplays a slideshow on an endless loop and include an option for a text caption at the top or bottom of the page.

2:Kiosk includes slide advancement controls available to the user on the right-hand (next slide) and left-hand (previous slide) of the screen.

3:Presentation includes a small slideshow controls box in the bottom right of the screen that allows the user to control the slideshow: advance, previous. Also at the bottom of the screen there are buttons to go directly to a particular slide.
