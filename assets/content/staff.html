Information Technology Services: Staff

Michael Baggett

Assistant Dean

Director of Information Technology

University of North Texas (Class of 1994)

Michael Baggett is a graduate of UNT and worked in private industry for several years before returning to the university as full-time staff member. He is the Assistant Dean and Director of Information Technology for the College of Visual Arts and Design. Michael Baggett oversees the IT operations of the college and affiliated institutes.
