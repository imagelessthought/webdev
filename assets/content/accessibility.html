Accessibility Statement

General

Updated: February 2023

We believe that everyone deserves the right to independence and equality. It is our goal to provide equal access to the information technology resources we provide and strive to ensure our services are accessible to all people, including those with disabilities.

Using the tools and resources available to us at the W3C Web Accessibility Initiative, we are working to meet the highest possible standards set forth in the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1).

Disclaimer

We are dedicated to continually updating and improving the accessibility of this site and the services we provide. We believe it is our responsibility and mutual moral obligation to provide access to all users.

We are committed to regularly adjusting and improve accessibility on this site. Our goal is to identify and rectify any accessibility barrier on our site. Although we strive to ensure all pages and content are fully accessible, it is possible some content does not adhere to the strictest accessibility standards.
